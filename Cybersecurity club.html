Who are we?

Web Development Club is a community aiming to promote knowledge of web development among students and establish a network of like-minded individuals who are interested in web development, computer science and programming.

Accomplishments

Web Development club is responsible for the creation of many different websites showcasing clubs, individuals and communites both on and off the RHS campus. We believe having an online presence is necessary in the age of the internet.

Goals

  • We envision a future where every club at Rocklin High School has its own website.
  • We seek to satisfy all of our clients needs and wants.
  • We hope that by connecting people online, our websites will help bring a deeper sense of community to the city of Rocklin.
